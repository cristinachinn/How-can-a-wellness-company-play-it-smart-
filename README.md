<h1>Bellabeat: How Can a Wellness Technology
Company Play It Smart?
</h1>

This case study is the Capstone Project of the Google Data Analytics course. I will be analysing data provided by Bellabeat to understand how its users are utilizing their services and guide the company's marketing strategy. The case study uses workflow proposed by Google consisting of 6 steps: Ask, Prepare, Process, Analyze, Share and Act.

<h3>Business Task</h3>
Identify trends in how users of smart fitness devices (Bellabeat and FitBit) track and use their health data. Use these insights to inform marketing strategies for Bellabeat products. Provide recommondations based on the analysis that could help Bellabeat better market their products to current and potential customers.
